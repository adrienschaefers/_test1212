Binary playfield

Duration : 5 days
Nb of participant : 1
Software : Photoshop

During this project two data were attributed to us:

- A place
- A cinematographic genre

From these data we had to imagine the synopsis of the film whose history is happening in the given place and which fits into the cinematographic genre chosen. Finally we had to create the poster of the film and decline promotional materials.

Synopsis

Dr. Bridge is a dedicated scientist working in Washington's research laboratory. As part of the "Phanès" project, Bridge manages to create a highly developed simulation of a parallel world, a computer program whose protagonists resemble us humans.
During the project he makes an accidental discovery: the inhabitants of this simulation develop their own consciousness. Being aware of the extent of his discovery, his fear of experimentation that some malicious people might realize the professor desperately looking for a solution to preserve the created life.
Will he manage to preserve them? If so, which way and especially with whose help?
