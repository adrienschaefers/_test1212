Aroma Zone

Duration : 5 days
Nb of participant : 1
Software : Axure - Adobe XD - HTML/CSS

The purpose of this project was to make the Aroma Zone’s website clear, modern and simple. We had to make the site as the image of the products it sells, that is to say: natural, artisanal, homemade and accessible to all. The first step was to produce a graphic model and then stylized with the colors and elements that seemed to be relevant.
